Smart Queue Management for Smarter Healthcare

SwiftQ is designed to simplify and streamline the management of patient queues in hospitals and clinics. It helps reduce waiting time, improve patient experience, and enhance operational efficiency. Patients can register and receive a token number, while the system automatically organizes and updates the queue in real-time. Staff and doctors can monitor the queue status, call the next patient, and manage appointments seamlessly.

How It Works

A SwiftQ helps manage patient flow and reduce waiting times. Here’s how it works step by step:

Step 1

Patient Registration

Patients register through a kiosk, mobile app, website, or at the reception desk.

Step 2

Token Generation

After registration, the system gives a token number showing the patient’s place in line.

Step 3

Queue Management

Patients are placed in different queues based on:

  • Department (like OPD, emergency, or lab)
  • Priority (emergency cases go first)

Step 4

Live Updates

The system shows live updates of the queue on screens or sends notifications by SMS or app.

Step 5

Patient Calling

When it’s the patient’s turn, their token number is:

  • Called on display screens
  • Announced by voice
  • Guided to the right room or counter

Step 6

Service & Feedback

After treatment, process is completed or patients may go to other departments or give feedback through kiosks or apps.

Benefits

Reduced Waiting Time

Efficient token management ensures patients are seen in order, minimizing idle time.

Improved Experience

Clear instructions, real-time updates, and digital displays reduce confusion and frustration.

Remote Notifications

SMS/app alerts let patients wait outside or in their vehicles instead of crowded waiting areas.

Transparency

Patients can see their position in the queue and estimated waiting time.

Priority Handling

Senior citizens, emergencies, and pre-booked appointments can be prioritized smoothly.

Organized Patient Flow

Doctors see a clear list of waiting patients and can call the next one with a click.

Less Interruption

Reduces unnecessary queries and interruptions from patients asking about their turn.

Streamlined Operations

Receptionists and front desk staff can manage patient flow with ease.

Reduced Manual Effort

Automated token generation and tracking minimize manual logging and paperwork.

Real-Time Monitoring

Staff can track queues across departments in real-time.

Detailed Reports

View analytics on patient flow, doctor performance, and waiting times.

Better Resource Allocation

Identify busy departments and allocate more staff or slots accordingly.
